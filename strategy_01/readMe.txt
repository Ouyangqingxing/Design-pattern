设计模式：策略模式。
实    例：超市收银系统。
解决问题：能够应对实际需求中如正常收费、打折收费、满额返现等收费方式，有较好的扩展性、维护性。
缺    点：增加一个新的收费方式仍需改动核心类。

DesignPattern:Strategy.
Example:cash system in supermarket.
Solve the problem:It include like normal,rebate,return kinds of cash and it becomes more extensibility and maintainability.
Disadvantage:When we add a new kind of cash,we also have to modify the core class.
